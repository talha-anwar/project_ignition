About

Project Ignition is going to be a crowd funded programme conducted by a bunch of high-school nerds. We are planning to design and build a miniature rocket capable of exceeding the speed of sound. We also look forward to designing the rocket-engine with our limited knowledge and resources.

To be honest, this is just the begining of the project. Also given the circumstances, our chances of success are really low as we are doing this in a third world country devoid of any social or economic support.
