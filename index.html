About

Project Ignition is a crowd-funded program by high school nerds aiming to design and build a miniature rocket capable of exceeding the speed of sound. We wanna prove that we can catch up to the world in terms of science and technology, provided we have enough support. We’ll show that we’re not a useless generation just wasting our time on games and social media.

To make things clear, we ourselves know that taking such a project in a third-world country like Bangladesh sounds utter foolish, and let’s not forget we’ll be trying to wrap our heads around “Rocket Science”. Furthermore, we’ll have to accept that we might fail horribly at any point in the journey just because of our lack of knowledge or resources. Even chances are that after actually building the rocket successfully with our blood, sweat, and tears, we can’t make it fly due to legal boundaries. Chances of failure are 99% or more.
